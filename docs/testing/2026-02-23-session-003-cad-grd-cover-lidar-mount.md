# Session 003 — CAD Design: GRD Cover & RPLidar Mount

**Date:** 2026-02-22
**Status:** ✅ Complete — Awaiting Parts for Physical Assembly

---

## Goal
Finalize CAD designs for two custom 3D-printed parts required for
Jetson and RPLidar integration, while waiting for the antenna and
standoff spacers to arrive.

---

## What Was Accomplished

### 1. GRD Cover — Custom Redesign (Finalized)
Completed the Fusion 360 design for a custom replacement of the Wave Rover's
original plastic electronics bay cover. The redesigned cover is dimensionally
identical to the original but includes the following modifications:

- Additional opening for access to the outer 40-pin header on the General Driver Board
- Enlarged OLED window for improved visibility
- Cable routing channels for Jetson WiFi antenna wires, allowing them to exit
  through the rear of the rover, where the antennas mount externally

Files uploaded to: `cad/grd-cover/`

### 2. RPLidar C1 Mounting Case (Finalized)
Completed the Fusion 360 design for a dedicated mounting case to securely
attach the Slamtec RPLidar C1 sensor to the rover.

Files uploaded to: `cad/grd-cover-jetson-mount`
